title: How to embed Reveal.js
---
It is very simple:

    <iframe width="480" height="400" marginheight="0" marginwidth="0" src="link to your reveal index.html">
      Fallback text
    </iframe>
    

Put it in a `div` to align it

    <div style="text-align:center">
        ...
    </div>

That's all.
